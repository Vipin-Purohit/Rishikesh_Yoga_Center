Yoga is a holistic practice that originated in ancient India. It combines physical postures, breath control, meditation, and ethical principles to promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. Yoga enhances flexibility, strength, and balance while calming the mind and reducing stress. It is a pathway to self-discovery, mindfulness, and inner peace, fostering harmony between body, mind, and spirit.
